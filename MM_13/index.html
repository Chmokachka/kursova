The purpose of Recurrence Plots (RP) is to give a visual analysis of trajectories in phase spaces. The appearance of the RP gives an idea of ​​the evolution of these trajectories in time. The phase portrait of the system (PhS) gives another kind of behavior of the system under consideration. A joint analysis of RP and PhS allows us to reveal new properties of the system. The use of RP for time series analysis makes it possible to determine its similarity by the available RP in this database - gallery.

This feature allows you to select the mathematical model corresponding to the selected RP. Then you need to perform parametric identification, based on real data. If such an RP can not be found, and the mathematical model of a real process is required for analysis, forecasting, process management, it is necessary to contact specialists.
